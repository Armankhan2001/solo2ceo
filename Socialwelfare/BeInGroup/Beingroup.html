Our Initiatives

Holistic Community Development

We focus on five key pillars to create sustainable impact in rural lives.

Women Empowerment

Vocational training in tailoring, handicrafts, and digital skills. We support financial independence through self-help groups.

  • Vocational Training
  • Financial Aid
  • Small Saving Units

Tribal Child Development

Integrated initiatives focusing on education, health, and nutrition. We run tuition classes and support ashram schools.

  • Free Tuition Classes
  • Nutritional Support
  • School Supplies

Farmer Support

Helping farmers during distress and training them in food processing to add value to their produce.

  • Market Linkages
  • Processing Units
  • Crisis Support

Healthcare

Addressing the disparity between urban and rural health. We provide emergency medical care and organize health camps.

  • Medical Camps
  • Emergency Aid
  • Hygiene Kits

Cultural Programs

Providing a platform for women artists to showcase their talents. Dance, drama, and arts workshops.

  • Art Workshops
  • Cultural Events
  • Talent Showcase
